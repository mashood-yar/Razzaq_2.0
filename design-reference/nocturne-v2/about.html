The Origin

A Dream Born in the Balochistan Mountains

Razzaq Luxe was founded with one conviction: that the rich, ancient olfactory culture of Pakistan — rose valleys, frankincense trade routes, saffron markets — deserved to be captured in bottles worthy of the world stage.

Our founder spent years training in perfumery, studying the raw materials that grow in the region, and listening to what Pakistani men and women actually wanted to smell like. Not an imitation of Paris. Something entirely, proudly our own.

Philosophy

Three Principles That Guide Every Bottle

I

Integrity of Ingredients

We never substitute. We source real oud, genuine rose absolute, and authentic ambergris — not reconstructed molecules pretending to be luxury.

II

Emotion over Formula

Every fragrance begins not with a brief, but with a feeling. Khan's Aura was built around the quiet authority of a man who never raises his voice.

III

Heritage, Not Nostalgia

We honour the past by building for the future. Our fragrances carry the memory of ancient Pakistan — but they are worn by people living fully in the present.

Journey

The Road So Far

2020

Founded in Quetta

Razzaq Luxe was born in a small studio in Quetta, with two fragrances and a belief that Pakistani luxury was possible.

2021

Khan's Aura Launches

Our flagship oud fragrance sold out within 48 hours of launch, establishing us as a serious contender in Pakistani perfumery.

2022

National Expansion

Razzaq Luxe reaches customers in Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad. Online orders exceed 2,500 per month.

2023

The Signature Collection

Seven fragrances — each with its own character, community, and story. Razzaq Luxe becomes Pakistan's most talked-about fragrance house.

2024+

The World Awaits

International shipping launched. The scent of Balochistan now travels to UAE, UK, and beyond.
